The Genesis Whisper

A Civilization of Unicorns, Pegasi, and Dragons, 8,000 Years Before Time

In the era before time itself was measured, three magnificent races wove the fabric of reality. Unicorns ruled from floating sanctuaries suspended among the stars. Pegasi carried glowing knowledge through wind-swept skies. Dragons guarded the elemental cores and shaped the very balance of magic. Together, they created a world where thought was language, light was architecture, and destiny was written in constellations.
